Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?

Traditional Indian attire is preferred. Ladies can wear sarees, lehengas, or salwar kameez. Men can wear sherwanis, kurta pajamas, or traditional suits. Please wear vibrant colors and feel free to accessorize with traditional jewelry.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate guests formally invited. If you received a plus-one, their name will be indicated on your invitation card.

Are children welcome?

Yes, children are welcome to all the wedding celebrations! We'll have special arrangements and a dedicated play area for kids. Please mention the number of children attending when you RSVP.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at least 30 minutes before each ceremony. For the wedding ceremony, we recommend arriving by 11:00 AM as the time is at 11:30 AM sharp. For Engagement and Reception, arrive by the stated time.

What about food preferences?

We'll have both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Pure vegetarian food will be served during the wedding ceremony. Jain food options are also available. Please let us know about any specific dietary requirements when you RSVP.

Can I take photos?

We encourage you to take lots of photos and share them on social media using our hashtag #PriyaWedArjun. However, please be mindful during the actual ceremony rituals and let our professional photographers capture those special moments.
