# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, collaborators, peers, and clients who visit Adithyan PR's portfolio to understand his technical range, project taste, and availability. They are scanning quickly, often from a resume, GitHub profile, or LinkedIn link.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Adithyan as a full-stack developer and designer from Kerala, India. It should make public work, skills, contact paths, and profile signals easy to inspect while leaving a distinctive visual impression.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, inventive. The voice should feel technically capable without becoming cold, and personal without becoming casual.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic beige portfolio templates, over-decorated SaaS landing sections, decorative card grids with no hierarchy, and motion that distracts from reading project evidence.

## Design Principles

Show real work before explanation.
Keep profile data scannable and credible.
Use motion as feedback and orientation, not spectacle.
Let the GitHub-inspired surface feel custom to Adithyan.
Protect readability across mobile, tablet, and desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Respect reduced-motion preferences. Keep keyboard navigation, focus states, semantic sections, and descriptive alt text intact.
